--- PAGE 1 ---
Sailors feared the open sea when dark clouds gathered low on the horizon ahead.

--- PAGE 2 ---
Thunder rolled across the plains shaking every window and rattling every loose shutter.

--- PAGE 3 ---
Old fishermen could read the weather by studying the color of the evening sky.

--- PAGE 4 ---
Raging winds tore the sails from the mast leaving the crew adrift and afraid.

--- PAGE 5 ---
Mountains block many storms but some slip through the passes with surprising and sudden fury.

--- PAGE 6 ---
Every storm eventually passes leaving behind a washed and quietly glittering world.
