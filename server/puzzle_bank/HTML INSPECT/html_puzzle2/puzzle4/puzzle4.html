Museum of Curiosities

Each exhibit holds a piece of the final inscription. Inspect the exhibits closely.

EXHIBIT 001

The Fossilised Wing

A perfectly preserved insect wing, encased in ancient resin. Scientists estimate it is 40 million years old.

EXHIBIT 002

The Speaking Rock

Visitors report hearing whispers when they hold this peculiar grey stone. The museum cannot explain the phenomenon.

EXHIBIT 003

The Arch of Passage

A miniature iron archway said to have stood at the entrance of a vanished city. What it guarded remains unknown.
