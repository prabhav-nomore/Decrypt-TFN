PUZZLE 4 — Museum of Curiosities
==================================
HIDDENKEY: AMBERSTONEGATE

Where to find it:
  Each exhibit <div> has two custom data attributes:
    Exhibit 1: data-fragment="AMBER"  data-order="1"
    Exhibit 2: data-fragment="STONE"  data-order="2"
    Exhibit 3: data-fragment="GATE"   data-order="3"

  In DevTools → Elements panel, click each .exhibit div and read the attributes.
  Combine in data-order sequence: AMBER + STONE + GATE = AMBERSTONEGATE

  Also hinted in HTML comment:
    <!-- The key is formed by reading the data-fragment attributes in data-order sequence -->

Technique: HTML data-* custom attributes
Difficulty: Intermediate
