Open morse_<WORD>.wav in Audacity. Use the Zoom tool to zoom horizontally.
Short pulses are dots, long pulses are dashes.
Use the Selection toolbar to read durations:
  ~0.12s = dot (.)
  ~0.36s = dash (-)
  ~0.12s silence = gap within a letter
  ~0.36s silence = gap between letters
  ~0.84s silence = gap between words
